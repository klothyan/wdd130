Here are some commonly asked questions we have answered for your convenience:

Q: Are life jackets provided?

A: Yes! We have life jackets for the whole family and more for free.

Q: How much is camping for one night?

A: Camping for one night costs only $15, but adds up for each night you stay there.

Q: Do I have to bring a tent? Do you provide tents?

A: Unfortunately you will have to bring your own tent if you intend to camp.

However, we do have cabins which can be rented for only $25 per night!

Q: How long is the river rafting ride?

A: The full duration of the river is roughly 4 hours, including provided breaks.

Q: Will breaks provide food?

A: Yes! Included in your package will be free food along the river.

Q: How far is the river from the camping area?

A: The river is about a five minute walk from the main campsite area, so it is very convenient!

Q: Are there fees if I arrive late?

A: No, we will not charge you any fees if you happen to arrive late. We understand!
